About Smart Farmer Portal

Smart Farmer Portal is an AI-powered platform that evaluates farmers based on their agricultural skills, farming experience, certifications, machinery knowledge, and verified agricultural records.

Instead of relying only on traditional credit history, our platform provides a capability-based assessment that supports fair loan decisions for both landowners and tenant farmers.
