About Us

About Smart Farmer Portal

Smart Farmer Portal is an AI-powered platform that evaluates farmers based on their agricultural skills, farming experience, certifications, machinery knowledge, and verified agricultural records.

Instead of relying only on traditional credit history, our platform provides a capability-based assessment that supports fair loan decisions for both landowners and tenant farmers.

🌾

Our Mission

To empower every farmer — whether landowner or lease-taker — with fair, transparent access to agricultural financing based on real farming capability.

🎯

Our Vision

A future where credit history no longer blocks talented farmers from growth, and agricultural expertise becomes the foundation of loan eligibility.

🤝

Who We Serve

Small and medium farmers, tenant cultivators, progressive growers, and rural entrepreneurs seeking collateral-free institutional credit.

Why Capability-Based Lending?

Traditional banks often reject lease farmers and first-time borrowers despite strong on-ground skills. Smart Farmer Portal bridges this gap by analyzing operational evidence — crop yields, land records, training certificates, machinery use, and market receipts — to build a credible Farmer Capability Score.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about eligibility, scoring, documents, and loan recommendations.

Who can use this portal?

Both landowners and lease farmers can apply. The platform is designed to support tenant cultivators who are often excluded from traditional credit systems.

How is capability calculated?

Your score combines agricultural skills, years of experience, profitability history, land size, irrigation method, insurance status, and verified documents such as yield proofs and sales receipts.

Do I need collateral?

No traditional collateral is required. Your farming capability, operational records, and verified evidence serve as the basis for loan assessment.

What documents should I keep ready?

Aadhar, PAN, land records (Khata/Fard/7-12), lease deed (if applicable), crop yield photos, sales receipts, and any training or certification documents.

How long does the assessment take?

Most farmers complete the multi-step form in 15–20 minutes. Your capability score and loan tier are generated instantly after submission.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All uploaded documents and personal information are stored securely and used only for capability evaluation and loan recommendation purposes.
