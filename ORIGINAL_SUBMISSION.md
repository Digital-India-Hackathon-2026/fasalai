## Idea
FasalAI — AI-Powered Credit Scoring for Unbanked Farmers
80% of Indian farmers have no formal credit history, forcing them to borrow from moneylenders at 30-60% interest. FasalAI solves this by generating an explainable "Kisan Score" (0-100) using alternative data — land size, irrigation type, crop diversification, mandi selling consistency, and FPO/SHG membership — instead of bank history.
Using XGBoost/Random Forest with SHAP explainability, the system shows farmers exactly why they got their score and how to improve it (e.g., "join an FPO to gain +8 points"), unlike existing lender-side black-box models. This makes farmers visible to NBFCs and banks for the first time, replacing predatory informal loans with fair, formal credit at 10-12% interest.
Built entirely on public data sources (Kaggle agri-loan datasets, NABARD/RBI reports, IMD rainfall data), the solution is realistic to deploy and scalable to India's Kisan Credit Card ecosystem — turning credit access from a black box into something every farmer can actively work toward.
